Stylo Pitch | For BBDO Pakistan. Stylo has established itself as a prominent footwear brand in Pakistan, renowned for its commitment to best quality and style. As part of its ongoing innovation to cater to younger, more modern active consumer segments, Stylo is introducing Stylo Active: a premium range of athleisure shoes for women in Stylo shoe ranges. These shoes are crafted for both style and performance, designed to meet the demands of the dynamic lifestyle of modern women. Building on the momentum from last year, Stylo showcased this collection through a vibrant and funky campaign that highlighted the versatility and fashion-forward design of the shoes.
