CHOOSE BETTER YAARRR

Client
VELO
Role
Design & Creative Direction
Year
2025
Scope
Retail Illuminated Displays + Digital

INFO

To visually communicate the concept of 'leveling up' we used the powerful, yet surreal, metaphor of a physical ladder extending past the known horizon and going above the clouds. The clouds beneath the marble pedestal and ladder signify both the new, unreachable level and the visual representation of smoke.
