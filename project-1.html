Weirdly Wonderful

Client
VELO
Role
Design & Creative Direction
Year
2025
Scope
Digital Campaign / Instagram

The Challenge

VELO empowers you to be unapologetically weird and uniquely wonderful to live life more vividly, even in the dull moments. Enjoy the Weirdly Wonderful, anytime, anywhere. The question was simple: how do you bring that energy to life digitally?

The Approach

We created a series of hyper-surreal key visuals scenes that shouldn't exist but feel strangely right. A tennis court dropped into a strawberry field. Office workstations set up at a picnic spot. A wrestler knitting. A hyper sloth charged up and ready to go. Each image pairs the mundane with the unexpected, making the weird feel wonderful.
